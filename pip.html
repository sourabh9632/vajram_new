Overview

Vajram NewTown-II

YOUR GATEWAY TO MILLAINAL HOMES 2.0

Following the resounding success of Newtown I, we're delighted to introduce Phase 2 - Vajram NewTown-II. Positioned on Thanisandra Main Road, this development represents architectural brilliance and construction excellence, setting a new standard for contemporary living. Expanding on Phase 1's accomplishments, Vajram NewTown-II offers spacious living areas with meticulous attention to privacy and luxury finishes. With panoramic views and unique amenities, it creates an unparalleled residential experience, transcending the ordinary and fostering a sense of community. Phase 1's success underscores our commitment, driving us to shape innovative communities in Phase 2, redefining modern living standards.
